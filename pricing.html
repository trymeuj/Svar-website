    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does the subscription work?

    Your monthly subscription gives you full access to all features of the Svar app. You can cancel anytime without any penalties.

    Is this a replacement for in-person therapy?

    Svar is designed to complement in-person therapy by providing consistent practice opportunities at home. Regular practice is key to speech development, and our app makes this easier and more engaging.

    How will I know if my child is making progress?

    Our detailed progress tracking system provides regular reports on your child's activities and improvements. You'll be able to see exactly how they're developing over time.

    Can I switch between different devices?

    Yes, you can access your Svar account from multiple devices, making it convenient to practice wherever works best for you and your child.
